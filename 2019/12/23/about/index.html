About Me

Hi, I’m Liu, a girl with 2 cats. ^._.^

I love design as well as the whole design process, it is of great fun to start from sketches and then create something functional and cool. I believe good design can add values to people and have the power to make a difference to life.

Currently, I am in the second year of master’s study in Human-Computer Interaction and Design at Aalto University, and now are actively working on my master’s thesis.

I am a quick learner and eager to get more practical experience as a UX/UI designer with passion and enthusiasm! Please contact me if you would like to work or speak with me! Thank you very much!
